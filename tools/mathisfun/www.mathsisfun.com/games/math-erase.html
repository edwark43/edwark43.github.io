Math Erase Game

Erase all the numbers by matching totals

 

In "add" mode the sum of all numbers next to your chosen spot must match its value.

Ignore 10s! So 6+6=12 becomes just 2.

In "multiply" mode you multiply all numbers next to your chosen spot, so 3x3=9
