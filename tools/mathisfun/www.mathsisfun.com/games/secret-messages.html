Secret Messages

Now you can send secret message to your friends ... using cryptogtaphy*.

Type your message, and your special Key Value (such as 1 or 2, etc), then press "Encode".

Your friends can decode the message when you tell them the Key Value.

*How does it work?

It uses a simple method of Cryptogtraphy (the study of secret messages) called "Key Offset".

Each letter in your message is moved forward in the alphabet a number of places (based on your Key Value)

Try typing in "abcd" and use a Key Value of 1, and you will get the idea ... I did say it was simple!

Just don't tell anyone how it works, or they may figure out how to decode your message!
